Medical Technology Center runs the project "Development and the clinical efficiency evaluation of vagal nerve stimulation method in the treatment of epileptiform activityand other functional disorders of the central nervous system (the device Stimulep)" co-funding within Sectoral Programme InnoNeuroPharm (Operational Programme Smart Growth 2014-2020), granted in the competition no. POIR.01.02.00-00-0023/17-00.
Medical Technology Center is research and development company established by experienced medical inventors and business venture managers that understand market opportunities and the challenges of building and efficiently manage innovative medical concept in field of neuroscience. We are focusing on treatement of epilepsy using sophisticated neurostimualtion technologies.

Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Lodz an international experts team in fields of neurology, neurophysiology, psychiatry, engineering, engineering and experience managers.
We are a privately-owned limited liability company (sp. z o.o.) headquartered in Poland, supported by an international network of specialists and a local team professionals, including academicians, technology specialists, business development, marketing, sales and administration managers in Lodz.
We combine a disciplined approach with direct involvement in the implementation process for each and every project; we partner and take the risk with our clients to initiate and deploy the best strategy to realize success. Medical Technology Center brings independent, high ethical standards and knowledge to the marketplace, continuously in search of innovative partnersin R&D and business opportunities that has a strong base in Poland.
