What is „e-Marinas”?

"E-Marinas" software has been devised to help with management of marinas. It helps providing service to customers incoming and outgoing ships, keeps record of ships in docking in marina and all sort of additional services provided by marina like slipping, yacht winterizing, showers, toilets.

"E-Marinas” enables its users obtaining full and easily accessible information about docking yachts and their payment status. There is a wide range of searching options including yacht name, name of captain, name of company, docking dates etc.

The System supports all kind of financial operations – receipts, invoices, payment records etc. All these documents can be printed, saved on hard disc or automatically sent to customer.

A very important and useful feature of “e-Marinas” is its reporting and informing module. The System includes many useful reports and is open to modifications reflecting customer’s individual needs. The System updates in real time information about amount of cash in cash register, amount of cash in possession of each bosun, current bosun deposits. Every report can be shown on computer screen, printed, saved on hard disc or automatically sent by e-mail to the office, if needed.

"E-Marinas" software has its own yacht charter service module. Built-in callendar assists reservation of charters; the System prevents reservations from duplication. This module helps serving clients – it records customer data, prints charter contracts, monitors payments etc.
