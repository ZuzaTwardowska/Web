Features of the system

Record Management of incoming and outgoing vessels – Port Book

  • instant and full information about all yachts in port
  • the system shows yachts with pending and overdue payments
  • simple and easy searching options including yacht name, captain’s name, dates etc.

Records can be kept in two ways:

  • On top of saving basic information about incoming yacht (name, captain, ship-owner, company, number of crew members, additional information etc.), customer can mark parking space where he wants his yacht to be berthed just by clicking on appropriate space on the map provided. This way user obtains graphic illustration of yachts' location in the port
  • Record of incoming yachts can also be used to save necessary information about each yacht without specifying berthing place

The system shows on-line number of vessels and their location in the port.

Payment Support

  • berthage payment records (cash receipts, proofs of payment)
  • payments records of additional services e.g. toilets, showers, yacht slipping, carpark etc. (cash receipts, proof of payments)

Deposit Support (deposits of keys, access cards, electricity and water cards etc.)

  • the system shows pending payments of each yacht
  • the system provides information about number and amount of current deposits
  • deposit records in all currencies

Invoices

  • the system automatically prints invoices upon receipt of payment for provided services
  • the system prints invoices for earlier provided services on basis of presented cash receipts. In this case there is no need to re-enter data – all necessary information are already in the system
  • the system prints invoices for any services provided by the port
  • invoices can be printed, saved on hard disc or automatically sent to client by e-mail

Residents Support

  • making contracts for residents
  • the system monitors termination of pending contracts
  • automatic renewal of contracts
  • drawing up payment documents for berthage (cash receipts, invoices)
  • the system reminds about pending payments or next instalments
  • automatically prints invoices when next instalment is due to be paid
  • automatically sends out invoices by e-mail
  • allocation of berthing places in a marina (interactive map of port with free/occupied places)

Payment Settlements

  • handling of invoices with extended payment date
  • monitoring pending payments
  • informing customers about pending payments
  • printing payment reminders or automatic sending them by e-mail to customers

Reports

All reports can be generated for any period of time (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annual) including among others:

  • financial reports ( invoice and VAT registers)
  • cash reports (cash in possession of each boson, taking amounts, revenues)
  • register of cash invoices
  • register of extended payment date invoices
  • register of invoices for each individual customer
  • revenue reports for all services provided in port
  • statistics reports
  • all reports can be shown on computer screen, saved on hard disc or automatically sent out by e-mail, for example to the office

Global Reports - "e-Marinas" is prepared to operate many marinas connected by computer network. This feature will allow to generate reports from all marinas working in the system in one place. Without leaving the office the user can obtain information about number of yachts currently docking in every marina. The user can check current amount of takings in every marina as well as for each bosun separately.

Additionally the System generates financial, accounting and statistics reports. As creators of "e-Marinas" we will develop reporting possibilities of our software and create additional reports suited to individual needs of each of our customers.

Database – "e-Marinas" has been equipped with set of databases of countries, cities, streets, yacht types etc. This helps and speeds up entering data as well as minimizes possibilities of mistakes. These databases are being upgraded every day which means they are well suited for the needs of each marina.

What's more, databases of customers, captains, yachts are also developed the same way, which speeds up everyday work. For example drawing up invoice for a customer that is already in database requires only a couple of clicks, there is no need to enter all data every time.
