Fiscalization in the system

"E-Marinas" is compatible with POSNET and NOVITUS fiscal printers. BIT Company has all necessary permissions to instal and service these devices. Connecting fiscal printer is very simple. All that needs to be done is physically connect fiscal printer to the computer, choose which printer you want to use in software options and... done!

Fiscalisation runs automatically. Entering any payment into the system (e.g. berthage, shower, carpark payments etc.) automatically produces a receipt for customer.

Any kind of cash register can be used with the System. While working with cash register, payment that is entered into the System is not automatically processed (this is due to the fact that it is not possible to connect cash register to computer). Such transaction has to be separately entered into cash register which can be a nuisance in a long run and may create room for mistakes (differences between report from cash register and report generated by the System). That is why we strongly suggest fiscal printers instead of cash registers.

There are plenty of finance and accounting systems available on the market already used in marinas offices. "e-Marinas" can generate files with financial information (invoices, cash receipts etc.) which can be later on uploaded into finance and accounting programs. There is no need to re-enter data into such programs.
BIT Company offers installa tion of "Symfonia" and "Optima" financial and accounting systems.
