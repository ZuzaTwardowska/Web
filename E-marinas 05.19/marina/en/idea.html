An idea of e-Marinas

The idea of “e-Marinas” has been masterminded by the BIT company owner – Piotr Żakowiecki, IT specialist and economist; yacht captain with passion who annually sails over 5.000 nautical miles in the Baltic Sea, North Sea, Adriatic and of course his favourite Polish lakes – the Masuria Lake District, Jeziorak, Solina and others.

"E-Marinas" software HAS NOT BEEN developed behind a desk or created by an IT theoretician ☺ . The idea of "e-Marinas" has been born on the water, on a yacht, while sailing from one port to another in order to find free space, open shop, fuel...
It has been developed in marinas during several, long lasting processes of registration at bosun’s, waiting for invoice, leaving e-mail or mail delivery addresses to receive invoice by traditional mail that sometimes never came.

The System was created to eliminate all inconvenience and difficulties. Thanks to this software we can be easily and quickly served at bosun's, invoice will be printed immediately or sent by e-mail. There will be no more mistakes with berthing time in the port, no more awkward situations when we could be requested to pay for the second time etc.
